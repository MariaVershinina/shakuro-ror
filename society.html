About

The Arthur W. Page Society has been made to connect and strengthen corporate relations. Being the world’s leading professional association for senior public relations, it’s large user base of Chief Communications Officers of Fortune 500 corporations allows corporate executives and educators enrich their professional connections.

Our Approach

We built Page Society from scratch from prototyping right to development, focus has always been to create a site that is direct to use yet feels delightful for the user. Our usage of best practices and responsive design resulted in a product that makes retaining and strengthening corporate relations simple.

Search distinguished professionals from our comprehensive directory

Easily find senior individuals from Page Society’s large members directory. Search by an individual’s name or by company and use our filters to quickly identify who you’re looking for.

View members by list to see photos and brief descriptions of an assortment of successful professionals or further refine your search with specific filters including location based search, individuals who are attending a specific event or members of a certain committee.
