Innovative Business Solutions

Organize.me is an efficient solution for businesses to arrange their document flow in a smart and ordered way that helps save time and money. The Organize.me platform offers a wide range of features from the premium scanning technology for mobile devices to a versatile Business API that provides automation of scanning and organizing tasks.
About

The idea with Organize.me and Scan.me was to bring modern technology into paperwork. The days of countless folders of paper, pens, and chaos are over. Organize.me makes managing your documents digitally easy regardless of your experience with technology.

Use Organize.me and Scan.me to digitize, edit, and manage all types of documents on any device. All the content is mobile integrated and saved in the cloud so that you can access it anywhere anytime. The featured professional editing tools on top of a high-performance scanning technology are simple, easy to use and effective.

Our Approach

We managed all the Organize.me product technical development stages. Working in a close contact with Organize.me team, we made sure we delivered the functionality that meets the goals of the project; that is create a revolutionary document scanning and managing tool.
