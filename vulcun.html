About

Vulcan is your one stop shop for everything eSports. Vulcan’s mission has been to make fantasy eSports, betting, playing or even just watching online matches simply hassle free. With Vulcan’s marketplace, there’s the extra dimension of adding to your in-game experience by buying new incredible items with your hard earned gold. Game changer.

Our Approach

We analyze drawbacks of competitor offerings and set to work on designing and writing the simplest interface possible while developing a fast, reliable platform.

GET YOUR eSPORTS FIX

eSports is massive and getting even bigger every day. So you better find the right game. We make that easy.

Our lobby system gives you filter and sort to find the perfect game, while giving you a quick rundown of what’s on offer. Whether you’re looking for that killer match with the massive prize pool or just the next great game to join. You’ll have no trouble.

WIN MONEY ON THE MOVE

Vulcun’s been designed to work flawlessly on both PC and iPhone, so you can always take part. On the move, or at home. No excuses.

Play and win. Or fancy your chances betting? Bet the gold you’ve earned and take your chance on the jackpot. Absolutely no limits.
